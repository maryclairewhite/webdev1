All You Need To Know About Me (and a little extra)

I was born in April of 1996 and a lot of super awesome crazy exciting magical shit has happened since then. This is the highlight reel.

I am an only child- a firstborn unicorn, if you will. If I was trying to brag I would tell you that I was everyones favorite in terms of grandparents and aunts and uncles. But I’m not trying to brag, so I will say nothing in regards to my status on the scale of relatives. I have been both sassy and sensitive since day 1. At age 4, I enjoyed music by The Offspring and Nelly and my favorite movie was Austin Powers: The Spy Who Shagged Me. Arts and crafts quickly became my thing and never stopped by being my thing. 16 years later, I am paying money for arts and crafts to be my professional thing for life at the Creative Circus. I was accepted to the Circus based off a portfolio of things I made from my freshman year of high school to my senior year of high school along side an essay about how obsessed I am with 90s music. It just felt right to write from the heart- and my heart is a dave grohl shaped space filled with glitter and carbs. I love bread and I love music. I will travel anywhere to see bands I like (or to buy bread). Some of the bands I have seen in concert are:

  • Foo Fighters (4 times! I was able to snap this picture of Dave Grohl, my hero, in New Orleans.
  • Red Hot Chili Peppers (twice! This is from the first time I saw them in Atlanta.)
  • Pearl Jam (I waited for 8 hours by the stage to be front row and then 45 minutes before they came on, I decided I’d rather go to the back to experience the concert with my mom. She’s my bestie for the restie.)
  • Soundgarden (Chris Cornell’s voice is so powerful!)
  • Better Than Ezra (Twice- once at Jazz Fest, once in Atlanta!)
  • Third Eye Blind
  • Bush (Gavin Rossadle was only a few feet away from me so I was too busy hyperventilating to take a photo…)
  • Dave Matthews Band (Vibing too hard for photography again)
  • Weezer (Twice again! Here’s them at Turner Field.)
  • Aaron Carter (and that moment is when you remember I’m 20)

I’ve seen a lot of neat stuff and done a lot of cool things. I have two tattoos, one of which I got on my 18th birthday with my mom, who bought it for me as a present. My mom is super cool and I’m sort of a carbon copy of her. At the same time, I’m similar to my dad as well. My parents have been divorced since I was two but in a cool way- the way where I have awesome step parents and my parents are friends and its not weird. Essentially, I have a giant family. I am also super close to my boyfriends family and this weekend I was asked to be my boyfriend’s sister’s maid of honor. My answer was a resounding hell yes because I LOVE parties. Not in a ‘let’s get wasted and make bad decisions’ way, but in a ‘matching plates and napkins and confetti’ way. Life is a special occasion and I’m addicted to the shindig. (rhcp reference for those who are still with us) I treat every day like its a holiday and my biggest source of joy is giving people presents. well, that and eating bagels. My 20 years has been a creative journey of discovering myself- a journey I will always be on- so my life story is more of a list of things about myself because all life really is is learning who you are and applying that knowledge to situations. I’m still young so I have a lot more to add to my story and I feel as though I am right on the edge of greatness. I live everyday excited to see what is going to be the thing that gives me that push.
